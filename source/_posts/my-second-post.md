## Why ShokaX?

ShokaX is a modern rewrite of the legendary Shoka theme for Hexo, featuring:
* **ESBuild Compiler** for lightning fast asset generation.
* **Modern Vue 3 Music Player** (Nyx Player) for beautiful ambient background music.
* **Modern Markdown Renderers** and advanced features.
